What is a Stock? Understanding Equity

Passage to Read: What is a Stock? Understanding Equity

A stock represents fractional ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you are buying a tiny piece of that corporation and its assets. The price is driven by supply and demand in the secondary market. The primary market is where stock is first sold (via an IPO), and the secondary market is where stocks are traded between investors. Understanding the difference between these is fundamental before you execute your first trade.
